Flat Iron Beef Steak

by Cocina JNOTS

Prep Time: 7 minutes

Cook Time: 8 minutes

Additional Time: 120 minutes

Total Time 135 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 flat iron steak (2 lb)
  • 2 1/2 Tbsps olive oil
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 Tsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 Tsp chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1/2 Tsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1/4 cup Cabernet Sauvignon (or other dry red wine)
  • 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 3/4 Tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 Tsp dry mustard powder

Directions

  1. Place steak in large resealable bag
  2. Stir together olive oil, garlic, parsley, rosemary, chives, red wine, salt, pepper, and mustard powder in small bowl
  3. Pour marinade over steak in bag
  4. Press out as much air as you can and seal bag
  5. Marinate in refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours
  6. Heat non-stick skillet over medium-high heat
  7. Sear and cook steak in hot skillet for 3 to 4 minutes on each side, until medium rare
  8. Discard marinade
  9. Let steak rest for 5 minutes before serving
