Meat Lasagna

by John Chandler

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 150 minutes

Additional Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 195 minutes

Servings: 12

Lasagna Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 6.5 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 Tbsp white sugar
  • 4 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 Tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 Tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 Tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 Tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 store-bought lasagna noodles
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • 3/4 lb Mozarella
  • 16 oz Ricotta
  • 1 egg

Go to World's Best Lasagna for additional information

Cooking Directions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a Dutch heat over medium heat until well-browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water.
  4. Season with sugar, 2 Tbsp parsley, basil, 1 Tsp salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and pepper.
  5. Simmer, covered for 1 1/2 hours, stir occassionally
  6. Bring large pot of lightly-salted water to boil
  7. Cook lasagna noodles in boilingwater for 8 to 10 minutes
  8. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water
  9. In mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, 2 Tbsps parsley and 1/2 Tsp salt
  10. Pre-heat oven to 190 degrees Celsius
  11. Assemble Lasagna:

    • Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in bottom of 9X13 inch baking dish
    • Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce
    • Spread 1/2 of ricotta cheese mixture
    • Top with 1/3 of mozzarella cheese slices
    • Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella
    • Sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

  12. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  13. Cover with tin foil: use cooking spray to prevent sticking or ensure foil does not touch cheese
  14. Bake in pre-heated oven for 25 minutes
  15. Remove foil and bake for additional 25 minutes
  16. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
