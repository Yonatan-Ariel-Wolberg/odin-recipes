Lasagna

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 150 minutes

Additional Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 195 minutes

Servings: 12

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Meat: sweet Italian sausage & lean ground beef
  • Onion & Garlic: 1 onion and 2 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato Products: 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: 2 Tbsp white sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles: stor-bought lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, Mozarella and Ricotta
  • Eggs:1 egg
