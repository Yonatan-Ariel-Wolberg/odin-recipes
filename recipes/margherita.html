Margherita

by Allrecipes

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Additional Time: 65 minutes

Total Time: 105 minutes

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 pizzas

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 package active dry yeast (1/4 oz)
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour for dusting
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • 20 slices fresh mozzarrella cheese
  • 20 leaves fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • sea salt to taste

Directions

  1. Stir flour and 1 Tsp salt in a bowl, then set aside
  2. Mix water, yeast and sugar in a large bowl
  3. Let mixture stand until yeast begins to form creamy foam (~ 5 minutes)
  4. Stir half the flour mixture into yeast mixture until no dry spots remain
  5. Stir in remaining flour, 1/2 cup at a time, and mix well after each addition
  6. When dough pulls together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface, and knead until smooth and elastic (~ 8 minutes)
  7. Lightly oil large bowl, place dough in bowl, and turn to coat with oil
  8. Cover with light cloth and let rise in warm place (27 to 35 degrees Celsius) until doubled in volume (~ 60 minutes)
  9. Punch dough down, divide into 4 equal pieces, and form each into a ball
  10. Pre-heat oven with pizza stone to 260 degrees Celsius
  11. Stretch out and pat 1 dough ball to form a circle 10-12 inches in diameter
  12. Place dough on lightly-floured pizza peel
  13. Top with 1/2 cup tomato sauce and spread to cover within an inch of the edge of the dough
  14. Arrange 5 slices of mozarella cheese on top of tomato sauce
  15. Place 5 basil leaves on top
  16. Drizzle pizza with 1 Tbsp olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt to taste
  17. Repeat for 3 remaining dough balls
  18. Slide each pizza onto pizza stone in pre-heated oven
  19. Bake until cheese is bubbly and underside of crust is golden brown (5-7 minutes)
